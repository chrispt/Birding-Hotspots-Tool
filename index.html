What is this tool?

Birding Hotspots Finder helps you discover the best birding locations near any address or GPS coordinates.

  • Find hotspots - Search eBird's database of birding locations within a 31-mile radius
  • See recent activity - View species lists from the last 30 days at each location
  • Sort your way - Prioritize by most species diversity or closest distance
  • Generate PDF reports - Download a printable report with maps, directions, and species lists
  • Save locations - Bookmark your favorite starting points for quick access

Requires a free eBird API key to access hotspot data.
